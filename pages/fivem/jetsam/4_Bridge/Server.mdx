# Bridge

## GetPlayerData
Retrieves the player data from your framework.

```lua
Bridge.GetPlayerData(source)
```

- source: `number`
- returns: `table`

## GetPlayerLevel
Fetches the player's level from metadata.

```lua
Bridge.GetPlayerLevel(xPlayer)
```

- xPlayer: `table`
- returns: `number`

## SavePlayerLevel
Saves the player's level in metadata.

```lua
Bridge.SavePlayerLevel(source, level)
```

- source: `number`
- level: `number`

## Payout
Adds money to the player's account.

```lua
Bridge.Payout(source, amount)
```

- source: `number`
- amount: `number`
